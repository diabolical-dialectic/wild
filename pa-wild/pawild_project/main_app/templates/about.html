ABOUT US

Here at PA-WILD it's our mission to make the backcountry accessable to all. To do this we teach outdoor skills that promote lifelong fitness, personal challenge, self-reliance and recreation. We strive to provide unforgetable outdoor adventure experiences. With our years of experience in the backcountry we can take you to places you never though possible and teach you the skills to not only survive but to thrive in the wilderness. We can do it all while documenting the adventure in ways that you wil be showing off for years. 10% of our profits are donated to organization dedicated to protecting and preserving Pennsylvania wilderness.


PATRICK FRANCIS: Co-Founder and President

Our frearless leader and the driving force behind PA-Wild, Patrick has decades of backcountry experience. His bushcrafting skills are second to none and there is nothing he enjoys more than teaching his craft to others.

A counsumate professional and outdoorsman with a tireless work ethic Patrick can teach you the skills necessary to thrive in the backcountry through fun and memorable lessons. Patrick's ability to find trout is legendary and he has been our most successful and popular Tenkara guide for years. Whether it's a multi night treck through the backcountry or a quick Tenkara excursion you are in excellent hands with him.

GAVIN FRANCIS: Guide

An alumni of the Quest outdoor adventure and recreation program at Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania, Gavin is an acomplished outdoorsman and guide. He has dedicated himself to the creation and implementation of exciting, outdoor, fitness and educational courses, at beginner, intermediate and advanced levels. He is currently working with PA-Wild to design special outdoor adventure experiences. Gavin's passion in life is to teach outdoor skills as a means to promote lifelong fitness, personal challenge, and to create indellable memories of outdoor adventure.
